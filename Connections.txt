ESP8266 (NodeMCU)        Components
-----------------------------------------
3.3V        --------->   LDR one pin
A0          --------->   LDR second pin
A0          --------->   10k resistor one side
GND         --------->   10k resistor second side

D2          --------->   LED long leg (+)
GND         --------->   LED short leg (-) through 220Ω resistor

D5          --------->   Buzzer positive (+)
GND         --------->   Buzzer negative (-)
