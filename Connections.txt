ESP8266 (NodeMCU)        Components
-----------------------------------------
3.3V        --------->   LDR vcc
A0          --------->   LDR DO
GND         --------->   10k GND 

D2          --------->   LED long leg (+)
GND         --------->   LED short leg (-) through 220Ω resistor

D5          --------->   Buzzer positive (+)
GND         --------->   Buzzer negative (-)
